track.findByAudioFeatures

Find tracks whose sound matches one or more audio feature ranges.

e.g. http://ws.audioscrobbler.com/2.0/?method=track.findbyaudiofeatures&filter[]=bpm:80:90&api_key=...

Params

pagefilter[](Required): one or more audio feature ranges that the returned tracks must match. Each filter should take the form name:min:mix e.g. filter[]=bpm:80:90&filter[]=percussiveness:0.5:0.6 will search for tracks with a tempo of between 80 and 90 bpm, and with an estimated percussiveness value of between 0.5 and 0.6.

bpm - estimated tempo in beats per minute
valence - mood estimate on a scale from -1 (very sad) to 1 (very happy)
aggression - mood estimate on a scale from -1 (peaceful) to 1 (aggressive)
avg_loudness - average loudness in decibels relative to Full Scale i.e. the maximum volume that can be encoded
peak_loudness - peak loudness in decibels relative to Full Scale i.e. the maximum volume that can be encoded
rhythmic_regularity - on a scale from from 0 (very varied rhythm) to 1 (very repetitive rhythm)
percussiveness - on a scale from (no percussive sounds) to 1 (many percussive sounds)
noisiness - the proportion of dirty/noisy sounds in the track, on a scale from 0 (very pure) to 1 (very dirty)
gearshift - indicates likely presence of gearshift modulation: 0 (no gearshift), 1 (gearshift by a semitone), 2 (by a tone), -1 (not sure)
key - estimated key or mode: see here for an explanation of the possible values returned
harmonic_creativity - on a scale from 0 (no harmonic variety) to 1 (many chord changes)
smoothness - rate of change of the melody and harmony parts on a scale from 0 (lots of fast changes) to 1 (very smooth)
danceability - on a scale from 0 (not for dancing) to 1 (very easy to dance to)
energy - estimated overall energy on a scale from 0 (very low energy) to 1 (very high energy)
punch - on a scale from 0 (not at all punchy) to 1 (very punchy)
sound_creativity - on a scale from 0 (very uniform timbre/instrumentation) to 1 (timbre changes a lot)
tuning - estimated pitch standard in Hertz, typically A=400Hz
chordal_clarity - estimate on a scale from 0 (very unclear harmonies) to 1 (very clear harmonies), key estimate should be accurate if this is high
tempo_instability - how much the tempo varies over the track on a scale from 0 (certainly a drum machine) to 1 (no recognisable tempo at all)
rhythmic_intricacy - on a continuous scale from 0 (ambient sounds) to 1 (highly complex), rock usually scores around 0.5
speed - subjective speed on a scale from 0 (very slow) to 1 (very fast)

api_key (Required) : A Last.fm API key.
